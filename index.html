User Guide

Learn how to use every feature of SuiteKeep

Welcome to SuiteKeep!

Follow these steps to get started with your concert suite management:

1

Customize Your Suite

Navigate to Settings and personalize your suite name and venue location.

2

Add Your First Concert

Go to the Concerts tab and tap "Add Concert" to create your first event.

3

Manage Your Seats

Select seats in the interactive firepit view to update their status and details.

Concert Management

Adding a Concert

  1. Tap the "Add Concert" button in the Concerts tab
  2. Enter the artist name
  3. Select the concert date
  4. Tap "Create Concert"

Editing Concert Details

  1. Tap on any concert in your list
  2. Select "Edit Details" to modify artist or date
  3. Make your changes and save

Calendar View

Switch between list and calendar views to see your concerts in different formats. The calendar view provides a monthly overview of all scheduled performances.

Seat Management System

Understanding Seat Status

Available: Seat is open for sale
Reserved: Seat is held but not yet sold
Sold: Seat has been purchased

Batch Operations

Use batch mode to update multiple seats at once:

  1. Tap "Batch Edit" in the seat view
  2. Select multiple seats by tapping them
  3. Choose an action (Mark as Sold, Reserved, or Available)
  4. All selected seats will be updated simultaneously

Family Tickets

Personalize family seats with names:

  1. Select a seat and choose "Family" as the source
  2. Enter the person's name
  3. The seat will display their name instead of "FAMILY"

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I reset a seat to available?

Tap on the seat in either the firepit view or list view, then select "Available" as the status. The seat will immediately return to the available state and any associated data will be cleared.

Can I track parking tickets?

Yes! Each concert has a parking ticket option. Tap the parking icon in the concert view to add parking details including price, quantity, and notes.

How do I delete a concert?

Swipe left on any concert in the list view to reveal the delete option, or tap on the concert and select "Delete Concert" from the menu.

Can I export my concert data?

Yes, navigate to Analytics and use the reporting feature to generate and export comprehensive reports of your concert data in PDF format.

Is my data backed up?

Your data is automatically saved locally on your device. For cloud backup, ensure you have iCloud enabled for the app in your device settings.

How do I track costs vs revenue?

When marking a seat as sold, enter both the sale price and your cost. The app automatically calculates profit margins and displays them in the Analytics dashboard.
